Services

Whether you are looking to improve your riding, or are looking to purchase your next dressage partner, Michiko Smith Dressage offers unparalled service.

01

Training: Intro to Grand Prix

Whether your horse needs the basics of dressage or is ready to make the jump to Grand Prix, our training program is built on classical dressage fundamentals that give your horse the best foundation in his training. Whatever the level of training, we provide custom dressage packages that are tailored to you and your horse’s needs and goals. Contact us for more information on the training packages we offer.

02

Riding Instruction

We offer custom tailored lesson programs to fit your goals both in the ring and out. Positivity and an encouragement are always apart of every lesson. Regardless of your goals and aspirations, we can help you meet them! Reach out for a list of our lesson packages and rates.

03

Sale Trials

Looking for your next dressage partner? With our sale trial service, we puts our extensive knowledge to use in helping you secure the perfect horse. Throughout the search process, we will assess and identify horses that will best meet your goals and wants in an equine partner. Searching for a top dressage horse can be difficult, but we smooth this process with our reputable contacts and extensive experience.
